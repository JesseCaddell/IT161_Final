Seinin

“Seinen” (青年) refers to young men between the ages of 15-24. Seinen anime and manga tend to be of a more violent and/or psychological nature than shonen series—though, of course, there are seinen comedies as well.

Blood and gore are common and themes can range from mild to extreme. They can also have content of a pornographic nature (though this is not the focus of the work).
